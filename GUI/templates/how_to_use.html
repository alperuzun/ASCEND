How to Use ASCEND

Follow these simple steps to analyze gene expression data and identify potential biomarkers.

Step 1: Upload Your Data

Choose a CSV file containing gene expression data. Ensure that the file follows the required format.

Step 2: Select the Cancer Type

Pick the type of cancer you are analyzing, such as breast cancer or lung cancer.

Step 3: Select Output Format

Decide if you want a CSV file, graphical visualization, or both.

Step 4: Let ASCEND Process the Data

ASCEND will analyze the data and provide insights into potential biomarkers.

Step 5: Review the Results

Explore the results in your preferred format and interpret the findings for further research.
